About

Lego is a toy company that makes bricks that can attach themselves to each other making unique designs. The word lego is made from the two danish words "leg godt" which means "play well". The Lego toy company waws founded by Ole Kirk Kristainsen in 1932. After Ole Kirk Kristainsen retired, He passed the company on to his son Kjeld Kirk Kristainsen. Over time, Lego has been one of the most played toys in the world as they are easy to play with and are very fun. Lego is cheap and very durable. These toys can be reused over and over again without breaking. As it grew larger, Lego have made new products like Wedo and Mindstorm.
